About

My name's Ashley and I'm a lash technician at SinLashes.

I took my first classic eyelash extension course in 2016 after graduating from the Social Service Worker Program at Seneca College. After reading #GirlBoss by Sophia Amoruso, I decided I was going to try and create a small business.

I took a risk getting into the field not knowing if I would enjoy it. But I definitely know now that I made the right decision. I love lashing and all the amazing clients I have.

I’m excited to improve my skills, continue learning, building, and making you all feel pretty.

See you soon,
💕 XO Ash
